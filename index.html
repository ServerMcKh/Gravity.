📋 SERVER RULES

🚫 General Rules

  • No griefing or destroying other players' builds
  • No stealing from other players
  • Respect all players and staff members
  • No spamming in chat
  • English only in global chat
  • No advertising other servers
  • Follow staff instructions at all times

⚔️ PvP Rules

  • PvP is allowed in designated areas only
  • No combat logging during fights
  • No unfair advantages or exploits
  • Respect "no PvP" requests in safe zones
  • No spawn killing or camping

🏗️ Building Rules

  • No inappropriate builds or symbols
  • Respect claim boundaries
  • No 1x1 towers or ugly structures
  • Build at least 100 blocks from others
  • No redstone lag machines
  • Keep builds family-friendly

💬 Chat Rules

  • No swearing or inappropriate language
  • No harassment or bullying
  • No sharing personal information
  • No excessive caps or symbols
  • No political or religious discussions
  • Use appropriate channels for different topics

🔧 Technical Rules

  • No hacking, cheating, or exploiting
  • No X-ray or unfair mods
  • No duplication glitches
  • Report bugs instead of exploiting them
  • No lag machines or server crashes
  • Use approved client modifications only

⚠️ Consequences

  • First offense: Warning
  • Second offense: 1 hour mute/temp ban
  • Third offense: 24 hour ban
  • Severe violations: Permanent ban
  • Appeals can be made on our Discord
  • Staff decisions are final
